About SCS-Invictus South Africa

Part of a global network delivering excellence in accounting, tax, and consulting services across 19 countries

SCS-Invictus South Africa (Pty) Ltd is the newest addition to the SCS-Invictus Holdings global network, bringing world-class accounting and consulting services to the South African market. As part of an international group founded in 2002, we combine local expertise with global best practices.

Our team specializes in serving Japanese companies operating in South Africa, providing comprehensive accounting, tax, consulting, and outsourcing services that ensure compliance with local regulations while maintaining international standards.

With our parent company SCS-Invictus Holdings Pte Ltd based in Singapore, we offer seamless international support and deep understanding of cross-border business operations.
